Quick Answers

Find instant solutions to the most common questions our team receives.

How fast do you respond?

Our standard response time is within 24 hours. Priority support members receive responses within 2 hours during business hours.

Can I register a store in any city?

Yes! While we are actively expanding, stores in any location can join us. We will notify users in your area once you go live in the app.

What if my app isn't loading?

First, check your internet connection. If issues persist, please email support with your device model and OS version so we can help.

Do you offer press interviews?

We love sharing our story! Please select 'Press' from the subject dropdown in the contact form for all media and press inquiries.
