Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change my plan later?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time through your dashboard profile settings. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle.

How do Mystery Boxes work?

Stores pack surplus food into a 'Mystery Box' which you reserve via the app. You don't know the exact contents until pickup, ensuring zero waste.

Are there any hidden fees?

No hidden fees at all. You pay the subscription price (if applicable) and the discounted price of the food you reserve. That's it.

What if I'm not satisfied?

If your Mystery Box doesn't meet the value standard or quality, you can submit a claim within 24 hours for a full refund or credit.
