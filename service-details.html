How the Mystery Box works

The exact contents are a surprise until you pick it up.

1. The Assembly

At the end of the business day, our partner shops take their untouched, perfectly good surplus stock and carefully package it into our eco-friendly boxes.

2. The Surprise Element

You won't know exactly what you'll get—it could be fresh pastries, sushi, or gourmet sandwiches. The surprise adds an element of culinary adventure.

3. Collection Window

You arrive at the shop during the specific 30-60 minute window listed in the app, show your digital receipt, and walk away with your discounted food.

Why choose the Mystery Box?

  • Unbeatable Value

    You are guaranteed to receive food worth at least 3x the price you pay on the app.

  • Maximum Impact

    The mystery format allows stores essentially zero-waste operations at the end of the day.

  • Culinary Variety

    Break out of your daily routine and let the chefs surprise you with their surplus specialties.
